BANANA BREAD RECIPE

Ingredients:

- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas and melted butter.
3. Add baking soda and salt, then stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
4. Add the flour and mix until just blended.
5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
6. Remove from the oven and let the bread cool in the pan for 5 minutes before removing it and placing it on a wire rack to cool completely.

Serve and enjoy!

Optional: you can add nuts, chocolate chips, or dried fruits to the batter for added texture and flavor.
